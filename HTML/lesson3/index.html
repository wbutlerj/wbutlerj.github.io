Purpose

Site Statement

Space Weather will provide weather information to all those that live on planets under control of the Intergalactic Empire! You WILL hear unbiased weather reports whether your planet is next to be targeted by the planet-killer space-station or not.

Many popular residential locations will be featured through links on the site navigation bar in order to provide weather information to all resident species including warnings on the ocational reactor blast or solar flare storm. Users can come to find their location and find images and temperatures for their respective residential locations.

spaceweathervdr.com

Audience

My target audience that can make use of this website are mostly men but also some women who live in Idaho. This audience will be mostly those who live in rural areas near water but also those in cities that shop at outdoor stores. Many of these fishermen will be middle aged but some are you and old as well. I hope this website can help those fishermen within these demographics but do not have many fishing friends to go out with.
