Target Audience

Audience Description

The audience I want to reach is any adult that lives in the galaxy under specific residential locations. This people will likely be under Emperial control and will most likely live in the following locations:Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven. These people will be clueless about the weather since many live in rural areas. They will be poor but this web service will be at no cost to them, provied by their generous emperor.

Personas

Name: Chirrut Imwe

Occupation: Protector of Kyber

Demographics: Lives on Jedha (rural and poor planet). Age: 35 Goals: Needs access to tell when he can work outdoors and not be shocked by kyber lightning.

Name:Saw Gurrera

Occupation: Rebel Fighter

Demographics: Lives on Onderon (rich but underdeveloped planet). Age: 55 Goals: Often has trades disrupted by sand storms. Needs to know when the sands storms will hit in order to better plan trades with raiders.

Scenarios

I need to know if it will storm on my flight to Canada

I need to better understand how fast hurricane colossus is moving towards Scarif.

What kind of damage did the tornado in South Carolina do?

When to expect flash floods in the desert washes of southern Arizona?

I need visuals of the density measurments of the storm blowing towards Rexburg.
